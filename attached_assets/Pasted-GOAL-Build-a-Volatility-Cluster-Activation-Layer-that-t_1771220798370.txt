GOAL

Build a Volatility Cluster Activation Layer
that turns your momentum system ON only when cross-sectional expansion is real.

Most days → OFF
Cluster days → ON

✅ STEP 1 — Define the Universe

Each morning at 9:30am:

Scan:

All stocks with:

Price ≥ $10

Avg daily dollar volume ≥ $50M

Premarket volume ≥ 500k shares

You can start with top 300 by dollar volume if easier.

✅ STEP 2 — Morning Gap Density Test (9:30–9:35)

Count how many stocks in your scan meet:

Gap ≥ 4% from prior close

Relative volume ≥ 1.5

Premarket high > prior day high (for longs)

Call this:

gapCount

Activation Condition 1:
gapCount >= 6


If fewer than 6 → regime OFF.

✅ STEP 3 — First Hour Expansion Test (9:30–10:30)

Between 9:30–10:30, count:

Stocks that move:

≥ 1.5 × 5m ATR from open
OR

≥ 2% from open with rising volume

Call this:

expansionCount

Activation Condition 2:
expansionCount >= 4

✅ STEP 4 — Regime Switch

If BOTH conditions true:

gapCount >= 6
AND
expansionCount >= 4


Then:

regimeActive = true


Else:

regimeActive = false


If false → your breakout strategy does nothing that day.

✅ STEP 5 — Optional SPY Veto (Secondary Filter)

To avoid trading ultra-dead days:

Disable regime if:

SPY 5m ATR < 0.8 × 20-day ATR average


This is optional — not primary.

🧠 Why This Works

Your February cluster likely had:

High gap density

High early expansion dispersion

Many tickers moving simultaneously

Your June–October period likely had:

Low gap density

Few expanding names

No dispersion

This filter separates those environments.
