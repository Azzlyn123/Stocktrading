What to focus on first

Do one surgical change first:

✅ Add a per-entry volatility expansion requirement

Because your biggest problem is no follow-through and your dominant exit is the time stop. That screams “entered during compression.”

If you add too many filters at once (ATR slope + range expansion + tier tightening), you won’t know what actually helped.

The first filter I’d add (ATR expansion)

You want something that’s:

simple

hard to game

works across tickers

directly attacks compression breakouts

Recommended ATR slope rule (5-min bars)

Pick one of these:

Option A (clean + robust):

ATR(14) now > SMA(ATR(14), 10) by at least +5%

i.e. atr14 / atr14_sma10 >= 1.05

Option B (slope check):

ATR(14) increasing over last 3 bars:

atr14_now > atr14_1bar_ago > atr14_2bars_ago

This is stricter but can be too restrictive.

Option C (split windows):

avg ATR of last 3 bars > avg ATR of prior 5 bars by +5–10%

mean(atr[-3:]) / mean(atr[-8:-3]) >= 1.08

If you want the best balance: Option C.

Tier tightening (do after ATR slope, but here are sane defaults)

Right now your tier ATR thresholds are way too permissive.

A good starting point:

Tier A: atr_ratio >= 1.05

Tier B: atr_ratio >= 1.00

Tier C: disabled until you prove edge

Where atr_ratio = atr14 / sma(atr14, 20).

If you insist on keeping C:

Tier C: atr_ratio >= 0.95 AND must pass the slope rule.

But honestly, your stats say C is just noise.

Range expansion filter (second change, not first)

This helps a lot, but it overlaps with ATR slope. Add it second so you can measure incremental impact.

A solid rule:

breakout candle true range ≥ 1.5× average true range of last 10 bars
(TR_now / SMA(TR,10) >= 1.5)

If you find you’re filtering too hard, drop to 1.3×.

What success looks like after just ATR slope

You’re trying to kill time-stops.

After the ATR expansion filter, your goal is:

Time stop share drops from ~70% of exits → <40%

Avg win rises from +0.20R → +0.60R or higher

Win rate can stay low, but expectancy must improve

You do NOT need a high win rate if wins get larger.

The one thing I’d watch like a hawk

Your time stop is currently:

“exit if not +0.5R within 30 min”

If you add expansion filters and still see tons of time-stops, then your entry timing is late (chasing), not just compression.

But don’t change the time stop yet—use it as a diagnostic.
