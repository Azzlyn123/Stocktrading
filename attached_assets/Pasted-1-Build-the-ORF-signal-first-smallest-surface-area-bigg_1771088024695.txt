1) Build the ORF signal first (smallest surface area, biggest payoff)
Define the Opening Range

OR window: 9:30–9:35 (5-min)

OR High = high of that window

OR Low = low of that window

Detect a “break”

A break is only real if it clears by a cushion to avoid micro-wicks:

BreakAbove: high >= ORH * (1 + breakPct) (default breakPct = 0.0025 = 0.25%)

BreakBelow: low <= ORL * (1 - breakPct)

Add a volume confirmation (optional but recommended):

bar volume > volMult * avgVol(20) (default volMult = 1.2)

Detect a “failure” (the trap)

Within failWindowBars (default 3 bars after the break):

For failed breakout (short setup):

price closes back inside range: close <= ORH

plus divergence: stock broke, but SPY did NOT break its ORH by the same rule

For failed breakdown (long setup):

close >= ORL

plus SPY did NOT break its ORL

✅ This is the “trap confirmation” gate.

mirror it.
