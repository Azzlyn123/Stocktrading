1) Fix the early-failure exit (it’s the #1 thing that can silently kill win rate)
Why

Your current rule:

if !partialExit && pnlR < 0 && price < EMA9 → exit

On 5-minute mega-cap breakouts, it’s normal for price to dip under EMA9 briefly during a retest/rotation. That rule will cut a ton of trades that later hit +0.5R. That hurts both win rate and profit.

Replace with “confirmed failure,” not “below EMA9”

v6.1 Early Failure Exit (recommended): only exit if weakness is confirmed by at least 2 signals.

Trigger early failure if ALL:

!partialExit

pnlR <= -0.15 (don’t exit on tiny noise)

close < EMA9

AND at least ONE of:

close < breakoutLevel (true level failure)

2 consecutive red candles (close < open twice)

“bear push”: current candle range breaks prior candle low (momentum down)

That turns early-failure into “this is actually failing,” not “it wiggled.”

Expected effect: win rate rises (fewer premature exits), avg loss may rise slightly but expectancy typically improves because you preserve a lot of +0.5R winners.

2) Tighten candle quality properly (your current gate is too easy)

Right now:

Close ≥ 75% of range

Volume > prior bar

That still lets in wick traps and average candles.

v6.1 Candle Quality Gate (strong but fair)

Require ALL:

closePos >= 0.82 (close in top 18% of range)

bodyPct >= 0.55 (body is at least 55% of candle range)

volume >= 1.25 * priorVolume (not just “>”)

Expected effect: fewer trades, higher follow-through, higher win rate.

3) Make the SPY hard gate smarter (keep it hard, but don’t block obvious single-name momentum)

Your universe is 14 names. If SPY is OFF for long stretches, you’ll get too few trades to learn and you’ll miss real single-name trend days (especially NVDA/TSLA/AMD).

v6.1 “Bypass” (very strict, should not hurt win rate)

Allow trades even if SPY not aligned only if ticker is screaming:

Bypass if ALL:

closePos >= 0.85

HTF bias aligned (15m) AND ticker above VWAP

This keeps regime discipline but captures rare high-quality outliers.

Expected effect: slightly more trades, win rate stays high.
