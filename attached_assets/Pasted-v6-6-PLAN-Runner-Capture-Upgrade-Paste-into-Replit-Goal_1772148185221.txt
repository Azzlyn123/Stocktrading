v6.6 PLAN — Runner Capture Upgrade (Paste into Replit)

Goal
- Raise avg win without killing win rate.
- Keep v6.5 entries + T1=0.40R + stall-tighten unchanged.
- Change ONLY post-T1 runner management.

Problem (from v6.5)
- WR is strong (~40%) but avg win is too small (~0.49R).
- Runner (remaining 30% after T1) is getting stopped too quickly (EMA9/prior-low trail is too tight).
- Need runner to survive normal pullbacks and capture bigger moves.

Changes (v6.6)

1) Post-T1 “Breathing Window”
- After T1 partial fills (70% sold at +0.40R), start a timer.
- For the next 15 minutes (3 x 5m bars) AFTER T1:
  - Do NOT trail with EMA9 or prior-low.
  - Hold runner stop at: entryPrice + 0.05R
  - If price hits stop → exit runner (normal stop_hit).
  - If price hits final target (if any) → exit runner (normal target).

2) Replace EMA9 Trail with 2-Bar Low Trail (Runner Only)
- After the 15-minute breathing window ends:
  - Set runner stop to the lowest LOW of the last 2 COMPLETED 5-minute bars.
  - Update it each new bar (ratchet up only; never loosen).
  - This trail applies ONLY to the remaining position AFTER T1.

3) Remove EMA9 from Runner Trailing (After T1)
- Disable EMA9-based trailing for runner management post-T1.
- Keep prior-low trail disabled post-T1 as well (2-bar-low replaces it).

Implementation Notes
- Risk per share: riskPerShare = entryPrice - stopPrice (same as existing).
- “0.05R” means: entryPrice + 0.05 * riskPerShare.
- “15 minutes” means 3 completed 5-minute bars since the T1 fill.
- Trail should use bar LOWS from completed candles, not intrabar ticks.
- Stop should only move upward (toward profit), never downward.

Acceptance Criteria (what to check after restart)
- Win rate should stay ~37–42%.
- Avg win should rise toward ≥ 0.60R.
- Expectancy should improve from ~ -0.10R toward ~ -0.03R to +0.02R.
- Fewer “runner stopped near entry” cases after T1.

Reporting after 80–100 trades
- Win rate, avg win R, avg loss R, expectancy.
- Runner outcomes: avg runner R, % runner stopped at BE+0.05R, % runner trailed out > +0.30R.
- Exit breakdown counts.

Do not change
- Entry filters, scoring threshold, SPY gate, VCS gate.
- T1 level (0.40R) and partial size (70%).
- Stall-tighten logic.
- Time-stop logic.
